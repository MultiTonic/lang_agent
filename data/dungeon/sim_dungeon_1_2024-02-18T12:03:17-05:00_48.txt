```less
description ::= "with a" type_name
action ::= "containing" verb | "guarding" verb
type_name ::= "weapon" | "spell" | "artifact" | "magic item" | "treasure" | "monster" | "quest" | "challenge"
verb ::= "trap" | "puzzle" | "riddle" | "obstacle" | "enemy"
exit ::= "the exit" | "a way out" | "the end of the dungeon"
```

The place can also be described in different ways. A description consists of the word "with" followed by a `type_name`, while an action consists of either "containing" or "guarding" followed by a `verb`.
